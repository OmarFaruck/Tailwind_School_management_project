NRB/International Students

Oxford International School provides the best facilities to ameliorate the life of your child. OIS welcomes NRB/ Foreign students with all those facilities to give them a friendly environment .

Based on the student’s nationality/ citizenship we take their admission on 2 subjects – English and Mathematics.

We omit Bengali from their admission text.

Any NRB/Foreign candidates are allowed to take admission in July [at the start of the session] in January [In the middle of the session].

They are specially taken care of to cope with the new environment. They will be given an introduction to the school and its policies as well as a tour of our facilities.

Foreign and non-resident Bangladeshi students are exempt from the requirement to sit Bengali O level. Instead, if they enter the school in class 8 or below, they can study German up to O level.
