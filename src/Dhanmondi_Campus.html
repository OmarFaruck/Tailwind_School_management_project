Oxford International School

Dhanmondi Campus (Main)

Welcome to Oxford International School- Dhanmondi Campus, a vibrant and innovative educational institution dedicated to nurturing young minds and fostering a love for learning. Located in the heart of Dhaka City, our campus offers a remarkable learning environment that combines state-of-the-art facilities, a comprehensive range of academic programs, and a rich history of achievements. Situated in a scenic and serene setting, our school campus provides an ideal backdrop for students to engage in a holistic educational experience.

We believe in empowering our students to become lifelong learners and global citizens. Our diverse academic programs cater to the unique talents and interests of each student, ensuring a personalized and inclusive learning experience. From rigorous academic courses to a wide array of extracurricular activities, we strive to foster intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and the development of essential life skills.

Message from the Vice Principals

We warmly welcome you to the Oxford International School, where every child is treasured as a precious gem in our community..

At Oxford International School, Junior Section, we foster self-assured, versatile students with a passion for lifelong learning. Our splendid education combines a captivating and relevant curriculum with a secure and purposeful environment. We have integrated the Cambridge Curriculum into our program, offering internationally recognized and stimulating education. This curriculum ensures a seamless transition throughout the years of schooling and provides students with the opportunity to succeed in a highly respected, externally validated world-class educational program. We foster independent thinking through Inquiry Learning, Project Based Learning (PBL), and Research Assignments. Our holistic approach nurtures talents, stimulates growth, and develops 21st Century skills, empowering students as future global leaders.

Thank you once again for visiting our website! If you are new to the area, we extend a warm invitation for you to come and explore our exceptional school. We would be thrilled to give you a personal tour and show you all the wonderful aspects of our campus.
